⚡ Welcome to Liquidated ⚡

🪙 This is a 3-match prediction game where you wager on whether certain crypto pairs will move 🔼 or 🔽.

🏆 You can either create a new game or join an existing one. Stake your NFT and if your predictions beat your opponent’s, you win their NFT!

🚀 Once someone joins your created game, it will begin 15 minutes later. If nobody joins your game within 45 minutes, the game closes.

🏆 Watch the live charts and see if you’re winning or losing. At the end, final results show who triumphs!

🔑 Don’t forget to connect your wallet and confirm bets. May the best crypto gambler win!
